About PawZen

Welcome to PawZen

Kerala's first comprehensive digital platform for stray animal welfare.

Our Mission

PawZen connects compassionate citizens, professional care centers, and skilled veterinarians to provide comprehensive care for stray animals across Ernakulam and Thrissur districts.

What We Do
  • Report & Rescue: Enable citizens to report stray animals in need
  • Professional Care: Connect animals with certified care centers
  • Veterinary Support: Access to qualified veterinarians
  • Adoption Services: Help animals find loving forever homes
Coverage Area

Currently serving Ernakulam and Thrissur districts in Kerala.
